Overview

"Supercar" began to appear and be used a lot since the 1980s. At this time, "supercar" mainly refers to expensive sports cars, with powerful engines and great speed. .


After a long time of development of the global auto industry, the concept of what a supercar is is gradually being added, changed and perfected. In the past, people often mistakenly thought that "expensive", "sports car", etc. was called a supercar. However this is not entirely true. A super car will have different and outstanding points, compared to normal sports cars and other popular cars.

Specifically:

  • The vehicle accelerates very quickly and has a very high top speed.
  • Using the most modern technologies in the world.
  • Special and impressive design, high brand value.
  • The selling price is very expensive.
  • The cost of raising a supercar is very high.
  • Produced in limited quantities globally.

Super car classification

For those who are "connoisseurs" and know a lot about supercars, supercars are classified into 3 main categories and sorted by high level as follows.


Supercar

Supercar are supercars with the following characteristics:

  • Reach 550 - 750 HP.
  • Vehicle weight is about 1,000 - 1,700 kg.
  • The maximum speed is about 320 km/h.
  • The acceleration time is 3s with the ability to accelerate is 0 - 100 km/h.

When it comes to the Supercar group, we can mention cars such as: McLaren 540C / 570S / 570GT / 650S / 675LT, Porsche 911 Turbo / Turbo S / GT3 RS / R, Lamborghini Huracan / Aventador, Audi R8 V10 / V10 Plus, Mercedes AMG GT…


Hypercar

Hypercar are supercars with the following characteristics:

  • Achieve a capacity of over 800 HP.
  • The maximum speed is about 350 - 430 km/h.
  • The acceleration time is 3s with the ability to accelerate is 0 - 100 km/h.
  • Must satisfy the criteria with only 2 seats.
  • The number of production to the market is less than 1,000 vehicles.

When it comes to the Supercar group, the cars can include: McLaren P1, Porsche 918 Spyder, Lamborghini Veneno / Centenario ...


Megacar

Magacar are the most advanced supercars with the following characteristics:

  • Achieving 1340 HP.
  • The engine is powerful and very high speed
  • "Rarely and unique", the number is very little counting on the fingers.

When it comes to the Supercar group, the cars can include: Rimac Concept S, Nio EP9, Bugatti Centodieci ...
